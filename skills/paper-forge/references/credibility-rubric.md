# Credibility Rubric

Use three lenses:

- Academic reviewer
- PhD reproducer
- Industrial technical lead

For each lens, identify:

- Strongest evidence
- Weakest evidence
- Experiments that should be added
- Largest risk

Use three credibility dimensions:

- Internal evidence credibility
- External reproducibility readiness
- Engineering transfer credibility

Allowed levels:

- High
- Medium-High
- Medium
- Medium-Low
- Low
- Unknown

Do not use fake precision such as `8.3 / 10`.

Explicitly distinguish:

- Strong conclusion
- Preliminary indication
- Unverified extrapolation
